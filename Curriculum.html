CURRICULUM

  • Home /
  • CURRICULUM

The programme of studies, is planned to give the students a general education in accordance with the aims and objectives of Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. The school is comprised of twelve standards. The curriculum includes English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, History, Civics, Geography, Art, Crafts, Music, Value Education, Home Science, Computer Science, Physical Education and General knowledge. Throughout the course, the social and civic duties, of a loyal citizen are emphasized.
