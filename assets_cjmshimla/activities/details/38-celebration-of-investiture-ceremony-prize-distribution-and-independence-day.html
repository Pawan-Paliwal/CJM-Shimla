Celebration of Investiture Ceremony, Prize Distribution, and Independence Day.

  • Post On: 14 Aug 2024
  • Category: Senior Wing

Moti Ram Arya Senior Secondary Model School, Chandigarh, was abuzz with enthusiasm and patriotic fervour as it hosted a memorable combined event on 14th August 2024, celebrating the Investiture Ceremony, Prize Distribution, and Independence Day. The event was graced by the esteemed presence of the School’s Director, Mrs. Rachna Mahajan, whose participation added to the significance of the occasion.
The day commenced with the Investiture Ceremony, a pivotal moment where the newly elected student council members were officially entrusted with their responsibilities. Director Mrs. Rachna Mahajan, alongside Principal Dr. Seema Biji and Headmistress Ramandeep Kaur, bestowed badges and sashes upon the President Boy, President Girl, Head Boy, Head Girl, and other student council members and class prefects. These young leaders took a solemn oath to uphold the school’s values and work diligently for the welfare of their peers. Mrs. Rachna Mahajan congratulated the newly elected council members and class prefects, encouraging them to lead with integrity and dedication.
Following the Investiture Ceremony, the much-anticipated Prize Distribution took centre stage. Students who had excelled in academics, sports, and various co-curricular activities were honoured with certificates, medals, and trophies. The school’s dignitaries congratulated the awardees for their hard work and dedication, urging them to continue striving for excellence in all their endeavours.
In her address, Mrs. Rachna Mahajan, the Director, expressed her pride in the students and blessed them with words of wisdom and encouragement. She emphasized the importance of leadership, discipline, and service to others, inspiring the students to carry these values forward in their lives. Her blessings and heartfelt words resonated deeply with the students, leaving a lasting impression on their minds.
Principal Dr. Seema Biji also commended the students for their achievements and underscored the importance of responsibility and a commitment to excellence. She expressed her pride in the students’ accomplishments and encouraged them to keep the spirit of learning and leadership alive. Dr. Seema Biji highlighted the importance of perseverance, reminding the students that true leaders are those who rise above challenges with resilience and determination. She urged them to be role models for their peers, to lead by example, and to embody the principles of integrity, empathy, and humility in their daily lives.
She also spoke about the significance of Independence Day, reminding everyone that the freedom they enjoy today is the result of countless sacrifices made by our forefathers. She encouraged the students to cherish this freedom and to contribute positively to the nation, building a future that honours the legacy of those who fought for it.
The final segment of the event was the Independence Day celebration. The atmosphere was charged with patriotic pride as students and educators came together to honour the nation on its 78th Independence Day. The cultural program that followed was a vibrant showcase of India’s rich diversity, with students performing dances, songs, and skits that beautifully reflected the spirit of freedom and unity.
The event concluded with a heartfelt vote of thanks, followed by the stirring rendition of patriotic songs, leaving everyone present with a renewed sense of pride and commitment to the nation. The day was a grand success, reflecting the school’s unwavering dedication to nurturing young leaders and celebrating the values that define our great nation.
