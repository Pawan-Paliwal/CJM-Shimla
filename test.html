Games and sports help to develop in children initiative, decision, endurance, skill team spirit and emulation. They build up confidence in their strength and a paralleled consideration for the weakness of others.

  • ✦ No child will be exempted from physical training games, unless a medical certificate is produced.
  • ✦ Those who are exempted from physical training or games must be with the class during the period.
  • ✦ The game's referee is the sole authority on the games field and her/his decision is always final.
